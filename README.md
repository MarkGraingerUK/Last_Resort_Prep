# Last Resort Prep Store 📦

## The Idea

I created this project to help the Last Resort Prep Store move from guesswork to data-backed decisions. When a store deals in essential preparedness supplies, being out of stock isn't just an inconvenience - it's a problem. I wanted to build a system that helps the business understand exactly what their customers need and where they need it most.

## Why this matters

As the store grows, keeping track of sales, inventory, and regional demand manually becomes impossible. This project is my solution to those growing pains. By setting up a proper relational database, I’ve given the business a way to:

* **Spot trends fast:** See what’s selling before it runs out.

* **Target the right regions:** Understand which areas are driving demand so the business can stock accordingly.

* **Improve the customer experience:** Ensure essential items are always on the shelf when they’re needed.

## What’s under the hood

I built this from the ground up to be practical and scalable:

* **SQL Server:** I designed the relational database structure to keep the data clean, organized, and reliable.

* **Power BI:** I focused on creating a dashboard that doesn't just show charts, but tells the story of how the store is performing day-to-day.

* **Real-world logic:** I treated this like a genuine business challenge, focusing on the data that would actually help a business owner make better choices.
